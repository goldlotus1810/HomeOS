# Q -- QUANTUM

symbol:    Q (→ U+269B atom symbol)
name_vi:   luong tu
branch:    R-reality

## Ngu nghia

Thuc tai o cap do nho nhat hoat dong theo xac suat.
Khong xac dinh cho den khi quan sat.

## Quan he

    quantum ≈ void         (superposition = all states at once)
    quantum → perception   (observation collapses wavefunction)
    quantum ⊃ energy       (quantum IS the foundation)

## Nguyen ly

    Superposition:  |ψ⟩ = α|0⟩ + β|1⟩
    Entanglement:   measure A → know B instantly
    Uncertainty:    Δx·Δp ≥ ħ/2
    Wave-particle:  same entity, different observations

## Quantum → Computing

    Qubit:       |ψ⟩ = α|0⟩ + β|1⟩  (both at once vs classical 0|1)
    Quantum gate: unitary transform on qubit state
    Speedup:
      Shor:   factor N in O(log^3 N) vs O(e^N) classical
      Grover: search N items in O(√N) vs O(N) classical

Python (Qiskit):
    from qiskit import QuantumCircuit
    qc = QuantumCircuit(1)
    qc.h(0)        # Hadamard → superposition
    qc.measure_all()
